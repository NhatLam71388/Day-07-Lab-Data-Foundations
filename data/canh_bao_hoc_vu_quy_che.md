## 1. Can cu va pham vi ap dung

Theo `VU_CTSV02 - Quy che cong tac sinh vien`, quy che ap dung cho can bo nhan vien, giang vien va sinh vien VinUniversity. Tai lieu quy dinh quyen, nghia vu cua sinh vien, cac hanh vi sinh vien khong duoc lam, hinh thuc ky luat, trinh tu xu ly ky luat, quyen khieu nai quyet dinh ky luat va cac phu luc/bieu mau lien quan.

Trong benchmark cua Member 1, VU_CTSV02 duoc xem la **policy chinh**. `Appendix IV - Procedure for Student Code of Conduct Incidents and Forms` duoc xem la **procedure/form reference**, dung de truy vet cac buoc xu ly incident, vai tro cua SAM/SDC, thoi han appeal va bieu mau FRM02.

## 2. Hinh thuc ky luat sinh vien theo VU_CTSV02

VU_CTSV02 chia hinh thuc ky luat sinh vien thanh 4 bac:

1. **Bac 1 - Khien trach:** Ap dung doi voi sinh vien lan dau vi pham o muc do nhe.
2. **Bac 2 - Canh cao:** Ap dung voi sinh vien tai pham sau khi da bi khien trach, vi pham nhe nhung thuong xuyen, hoac vi pham lan dau tuong doi nghiem trong.
3. **Bac 3 - Dinh chi hoc tap co thoi han:** Ap dung voi sinh vien dang trong thoi gian bi canh cao nhung tiep tuc vi pham, hoac vi pham nghiem trong cac hanh vi bi cam. Thoi gian dinh chi co the la mot hoc ky, mot nam hoc, hoac theo truong hop cu the do lanh dao nha truong quyet dinh.
4. **Bac 4 - Buoc thoi hoc:** Ap dung voi sinh vien dang trong thoi gian bi dinh chi hoc tap ma tiep tuc vi pham, hoac vi pham lan dau co tinh chat dac biet nghiem trong, gay thiet hai dang ke cho nha truong hoac xa hoi, hoac bi ket an tu ma khong duoc huong an treo.

Tu muc canh cao tro len, hinh thuc ky luat phai duoc luu vao ho so quan ly sinh vien va thong bao cho gia dinh sinh vien. Neu sinh vien bi dinh chi hoc tap co thoi han hoac buoc thoi hoc, co so giao duc dai hoc phai gui thong bao cho dia phuong va gia dinh sinh vien de phoi hop quan ly, giao duc.

## 3. Trinh tu, thu tuc va hoi dong xu ly ky luat theo VU_CTSV02

Theo VU_CTSV02, doi voi cac hanh vi vi pham ky luat duoc phan loai **Bac 1 hoac Bac 2**, Phong Cong tac Sinh vien co trach nhiem xem xet vu viec, danh gia bang chung va quyet dinh bien phap ky luat thich hop.

Doi voi hanh vi vi pham duoc phan loai **Bac 3 hoac Bac 4**, Hoi dong khen thuong va ky luat sinh vien se trieu tap cuoc hop de danh gia vu viec va de xuat bien phap ky luat len Hieu truong de phe duyet cuoi cung.

Thanh phan Hoi dong khen thuong va ky luat sinh vien bao gom:

- Chu tich: Pho Hieu truong Van hanh hoc thuat.
- Pho Chu tich/uy vien thuong truc: Truong phong Cong tac Sinh vien.
- Cac thanh vien: dai dien cac phong ban chuyen mon, phong hanh chinh, Doan Thanh nien Cong san Ho Chi Minh va/hoac Hoi sinh vien.
- Hoi dong co the moi dai dien nhom, giam doc chuong trinh hoac co van hoc thuat tham du hop de cung cap thong tin va khuyen nghi, nhung nhung nguoi nay khong co quyen bieu quyet trong quyet dinh cuoi cung.

VU_CTSV02 cung chi ro rang cac buoc xu ly vi pham Quy che sinh vien va bieu mau lien quan duoc tham chieu tai Phu luc 4.

## 4. Quyen khieu nai quyet dinh ky luat theo VU_CTSV02

Neu sinh vien cho rang quyet dinh ky luat la khong cong bang hoac khong chinh xac, sinh vien co quyen nop don khieu nai len Hoi dong khen thuong va ky luat sinh vien.

Sinh vien phai gui email bao gom don khieu nai va minh chung cu the toi dia chi `conduct@vinuni.edu.vn` trong vong **10 ngay lam viec** ke tu ngay nhan duoc quyet dinh ky luat.

Hoi dong khen thuong va ky luat sinh vien se xem xet lai vu viec. Chu tich Hoi dong co the quyet dinh moi them thanh vien moi tham gia cac buoc xem xet lai neu can thiet.

## 5. Bieu mau va tai lieu lien quan theo VU_CTSV02

VU_CTSV02 liet ke cac bieu mau va tai lieu lien quan den quy trinh xu ly vi pham sinh vien, bao gom:

- **FRM01:** Mau bao cao hanh vi sai trai bi nghi ngo.
- **FRM02:** Mau bao cao vi pham cua sinh vien.
- Quy dinh ve Hanh vi tinh duc sai trai va xu ly.

Trong benchmark cua Member 1, FRM02 duoc truy vet them qua Appendix IV, trong do FRM02 duoc ghi la **Form 2 - Student Misconduct Report Form**.

## 6. Appendix IV: Level 3/Level 4, SDC, approval va appeal process

Theo `Appendix IV - Procedure for Student Code of Conduct Incidents and Forms`, cac tu viet tat va bieu mau chinh gom:

- **SAM:** Student Affairs Management.
- **SDC:** Student Disciplinary Committee.
- **Form 1 (FRM01):** Suspected Misconduct Report Form.
- **Form 2 (FRM02):** Student Misconduct Report Form.

Trong workflow xu ly Student Code of Conduct incidents:

- Doi voi **Level 1 va Level 2**, SAM xac dinh huong giai quyet va sanction cua vu viec.
- Doi voi **Level 3 va Level 4**, SAM dua vu viec len **SDC** de thao luan va ra quyet dinh.
- O giai doan Level 3/Level 4, viec hop voi sinh vien co the duoc yeu cau de thong bao va lam ro quyet dinh.
- Trong vong 5 working days ke tu ngay vi pham, thu chinh thuc duoc gui tu `conduct@vinuni.edu.vn` va sinh vien duoc moi hop de thong bao quyet dinh doi voi Level 3 va Level 4.
- Neu quyet dinh la **suspension** hoac **dismissal**, Committee phai xin approval cua **Provost hoac President**, va phu huynh sinh vien se duoc thong bao.
- Neu sinh vien khong dong y voi quyet dinh, sinh vien co the **appeal within 10 working days after the decision is announced**.
- Quy trinh appeal su dung **FRM02 - Form 2, Student Misconduct Report Form**.
- Toan bo allegations of misconduct va outcomes phai duoc document/archive tai SAM Office va nhap vao Student Code of Conduct database.
